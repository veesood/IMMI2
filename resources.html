Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Australian visas and our services.

Student visa processing times vary depending on the assessment level of your country and the completeness of your application. Generally, it takes between 1 to 3 months. We recommend applying at least 3 months before your course starts to allow for any unexpected delays.

For skilled migration visas, you typically need:

  • Passport and identity documents
  • Skills assessment results
  • English language test results
  • Educational qualifications
  • Work experience evidence
  • Health and character documents

Download our detailed checklist for a complete list of required documents.

The current government application fee for a partner visa (subclass 820/801) is AUD 8,850. Additional costs may include health examinations, police checks, and translation of documents. Our professional service fees for partner visa applications start from $4,999.
